About Me

Hello! I'm Shahriar Kamal, a second-year Electrical Engineering student at KUET. My journey is driven by a deep fascination with turning complex ideas into tangible, working devices. I thrive at the intersection of hardware and software—designing circuits, laying out custom PCBs, and writing the code that gives them intelligence. Beyond the lab, I apply the same 'builder' mindset to creating communities and leading projects, believing that great technology and great teams share the principles of clear vision and strong execution.
